### ON THE INTELLECTUAL LINEAGE OF THE IFMP

The IFMP draws on:

- **Georgism** (Henry George): the socialization of land-rent via Land Value Capture.
- **Mutualism** (Pierre-Joseph Proudhon): cost-basis credit, worker ownership, and federalism.
- **Gesellian Free-Money** (Silvio Gesell): the critique of interest-bearing money.
- **Commons Governance** (Elinor Ostrom): polycentric, non-state governance of shared resources.
- **The Cooperative Movement**: worker-owned enterprises, mutual banks, and community land trusts.
